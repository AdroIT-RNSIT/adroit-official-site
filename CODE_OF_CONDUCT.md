## Our Commitment

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

We aim to foster an open and collaborative environment focused on constructive learning and development.

---

## Expected Behaviour

Participants are expected to:

* Be respectful and professional in communication.
* Provide constructive feedback.
* Accept responsibility for mistakes and learn from them.
* Focus on improving the project collaboratively.
* Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

The following behaviours are not tolerated:

* Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments.
* Personal attacks or trolling.
* Publishing private information without permission.
* Spam or disruptive behaviour.
* Any conduct that negatively affects collaboration.

---

## Communication Guidelines

* Keep discussions technical and objective.
* Provide clear explanations when suggesting changes.
* Avoid hostile or dismissive language.
* Assume positive intent from contributors.

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

* Clarifying and enforcing standards.
* Reviewing reported issues.
* Taking appropriate and fair corrective action.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour:

* Open a private issue or contact the maintainers directly.
* Provide clear details so the situation can be reviewed.

All reports will be handled respectfully and confidentially.

---

## Enforcement Actions

Maintainers may take actions including:

* Warning the contributor.
* Removing content.
* Temporarily or permanently restricting participation.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

* GitHub discussions
* Issues and Pull Requests
* Project communication channels
