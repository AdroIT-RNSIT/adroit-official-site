# AdroIT Membership

## How to Join?
To become a member, you can register through our official website during the recruitment drive which usually happens at the beginning of the odd semester.

## Benefits
- Access to exclusive workshops and seminars.
- Hands-on projects in your chosen domain.
- Network with seniors and alumni.
- Certificate of appreciation upon active participation.
- Opportunity to become a core team member in subsequent years.

## FAQ
**Q: Is there a membership fee?**
A: No, membership is free for all selected students.

**Q: Can I change my domain later?**
A: Yes, based on your performance and interest, domain changes are allowed after one semester.

**Q: Can non-members access resources?**
A: No, you'll need to be an AdroIT member to access resources
