# About AdroIT

AdroIT is the official technical club of the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at RNSIT. Founded in 2020, we are a community of tech enthusiasts dedicated to learning, building, and growing together.

## Our Mission
To foster a culture of innovation and technical excellence among students. We organize workshops, hackathons, and seminars to help students bridge the gap between academic curriculum and industry requirements.

## Domains
We have 4 main technical domains:
1. **Machine Learning (ML)**: Exploring AI, Deep Learning, and data science.
2. **Cloud Computing (CC)**: Learning about AWS, Azure, Docker, and Kubernetes.
3. **Cybersecurity (CY)**: Ethical hacking, network security, and CTFs.
4. **Data Analytics (DA)**: Big data, visualization, and business intelligence.
