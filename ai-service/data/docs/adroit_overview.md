# ADROIT CLUB

## 1. Overview: What is Adroit Club?

Adroit Club is a student-led technical and innovation-focused club that
operates within a college campus environment. The club is dedicated to:

-   Promoting technical excellence
-   Encouraging innovation and problem-solving
-   Organizing workshops, hackathons, and tech events
-   Building real-world project experience among students
-   Preparing members for industry-level skills and professional growth

### Purpose of the Club

The primary objectives of Adroit Club are:

-   To bridge the gap between academic learning and industry
    requirements
-   To create a collaborative environment for building impactful
    projects
-   To mentor students in areas such as:
    -   Programming
    -   Web/App Development
    -   AI/ML
    -   Data Structures & Algorithms
    -   Cloud Computing

Adroit functions as both a learning platform and an execution team for
technical initiatives.
